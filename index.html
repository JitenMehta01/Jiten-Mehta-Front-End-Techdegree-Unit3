LCFC

Leicester City Football Club is an English professional football club based in Leicester.

The club competes in the Premier League, England's top division of football, and plays its home games at the King Power Stadium.

Leicester City won the 2015–16 Premier League, their first top-level football championship.

They are one of only six clubs to have won the Premier League since its inception in 1992. A number of newspapers described their title win as the greatest sporting shock ever, considering at the start of the season they were favourites to face relegation.
